In the task parameters panel on the left, under **3D Outputs**, select the desired reconstruction results and corresponding output formats.

![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/成果及格式勾选设置.webp)

Click the icon on the right side of **Output Coordinate System** to open the coordinate system settings interface.

![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/成果坐标系.webp)

You can configure the **horizontal coordinate system** and **vertical (elevation) coordinate system**. You may also enable a **custom origin** if needed. After completing the settings, click **Confirm**.

![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/成果坐标系设置.webp)

![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/成果高程坐标系.webp)

After all configurations are complete, click **Start Reconstruction** to begin the task.

![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/开始重建.webp)
