Click **Edit Camera** above the image list to view and modify the corresponding camera parameters.

![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/编辑相机功能.webp)

LiDAR systems typically use fisheye cameras. Select **Fisheye** as the camera type, then set the corresponding focal length, principal point (X, Y), distortion parameters, and other required values.

![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/相机参数设置.webp)

The configured camera parameters can be saved to the local database by clicking **Save to Local Database**.

![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/相机参数保存到本地数据库.webp)

You can also click **Select from Database** ![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/从数据库中选择.webp) to apply previously saved camera parameters.

![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/选择相机参数.webp)

Use the camera list on the left to switch between cameras and configure their settings. After all camera parameters are set, click **Confirm**.

![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/切换设置相机.webp)
