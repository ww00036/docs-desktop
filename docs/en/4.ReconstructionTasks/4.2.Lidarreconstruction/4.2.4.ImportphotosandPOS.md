### Import Photos
In the task parameters panel on the left, you can import image data by **file** or by **folder**.

- **Import by file** allows you to select specific images within a folder to participate in reconstruction.  
- **Import by folder** will import all images within the selected folder (including subfolders) into the task.

![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/导入照片方式.webp)

If the images contain camera parameters and positioning information, the software will automatically read them.

![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/照片信息读取.webp)

**Note:** Fisheye images from LiDAR systems must be grouped by camera and imported according to their respective cameras.

![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/导入照片区分相机.webp)

After importing images, you can click the corresponding function above the image list to continue importing more data. You can also click the delete icon on the right side to remove images from a camera.

![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/继续导入照片及删除.webp)

### Import POS

If the POS data is incorrect or needs modification, click the **POS** function above the image list.

![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/POS功能.webp)

Click **Import POS File** ![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/导入POS文件.webp), select the corresponding POS file, choose the attitude angle convention used, and assign the appropriate columns for name, XYZ coordinates, and attitude angles. After configuration, click **Confirm**.

![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/设置姿态角.webp)

![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/设置POS列名.webp)

Next, set the coordinate system for the POS data ![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/POS坐标系.webp).

![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/POS坐标系设置.webp)

Then set the coordinate accuracy. For LiDAR datasets, **high accuracy** is generally recommended.

![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/POS坐标精度.webp)

After completing the setup, click **Confirm**. If there are incorrect or unnecessary parts, you can use the **Region Delete** function (left-click to draw a selection area, double-click to finish) to remove the corresponding POS and images.

![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/区域删除功能.webp)

![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/区域删除.webp)

If configured correctly, a preview will be displayed on the map after import.

![](../../img/desktop-manual-en/4.2/POS预览结果.webp)
