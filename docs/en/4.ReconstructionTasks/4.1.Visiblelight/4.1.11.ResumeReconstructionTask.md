### Continuing Reconstruction for Additional Output Formats
After the initial reconstruction of 3D results, you can add more output formats. This means you can continue the reconstruction based on the first set of results to generate new formats. For example, if you only selected the OSGB format during the first reconstruction, after it's complete, you can then select the OBJ format, click "Start Reconstruction," and after a quick continuation process, you will obtain the results in OBJ format.

### Continuing Reconstruction for Different Coordinate Systems
After the initial reconstruction of 2D and 3D results, you can modify the output coordinate system. This allows you to continue the reconstruction based on the first set of results to obtain outputs in the newly specified coordinate system. For example, if you initially selected a local coordinate system (e.g., East-North-Up), after reconstruction, you can change it to WGS 84 / UTM zone 50N. Click "Start Reconstruction," and after a quick continuation process, you will get the results in the WGS 84 / UTM zone 50N coordinate system.
